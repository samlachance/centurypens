Century Pens has been Chicago's premier pen shop for over 30 years. Known for our gigantic selection from over 30 manufacturers and our 56 years of combined retail pen experience, we serve as Chicago's definitive pen source.

Ed Hamilton, affectionately known as 'Mr. Pen', has dedicated his professional life to fine writing instruments. Prior to the opening of Century Pens, Ed opened seven pen shops in the Chicago area making him one of the most ambitious writing instrument experts in the country.
